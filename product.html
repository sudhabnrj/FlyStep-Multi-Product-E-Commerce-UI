Product Overview

Engineered with top-grade materials for optimal performance, ensuring lightweight velocity, structural arch support, and comfortable shock distribution through high-impact runs, trails, or street sessions.

Engineered Kinetics & Tech

Features FlyStep's proprietary carbon fiber propulsion plate embedded within the midsole, acting as a springboard for forward momentum. The AeroCore foam layout maximizes energy return by up to 85%, reducing runner muscle fatigue during long-distance training.

Materials Specifications

Upper: 90% recycled engineered knit fabric offering seamless stretch zones. Midsole: Bio-sourced polyurethane cushioning compound. Outsole: Carbon-injected rubber compound for durable wear and high traction levels.

Recommended Usage

Care Instructions
  • Do not machine wash or tumble dry. Hand-clean only.
  • Use mild soap and cold water with a soft-bristled brush to remove dirt.
  • Air-dry in shade. Avoid direct sunlight or high heat exposure.
  • Remove inner insoles before washing for faster dry times.
