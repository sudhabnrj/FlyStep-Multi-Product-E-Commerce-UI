# Observation - Iteration 1

## Overall

The Product Detail Page follows the planned structure and presents the product information in a clear, well-organized layout.

The page maintains visual consistency with the Landing Page and Product Listing Page while providing a premium shopping experience.

The product gallery, product information, purchase actions, and supporting sections create a logical purchase flow.

---

## Strengths

✓ Product gallery is visually dominant.

✓ Product images are high quality.

✓ Product information hierarchy is clear.

✓ Price, discount, and rating are easy to identify.

✓ Size and color selection components are intuitive.

✓ Add to Cart and Buy Now buttons are clearly visible.

✓ Trust badges increase purchase confidence.

✓ Product Highlights are easy to scan.

✓ Specifications table is clean and readable.

✓ Customer Reviews improve credibility.

✓ FAQ accordion works correctly.

✓ Related Products section encourages additional browsing.

✓ Recently Viewed section maintains continuity.

✓ Newsletter and Footer remain consistent with previous pages.

✓ Responsive layout performs well on desktop and mobile.

---

## Weaknesses

- Product gallery transition feels basic.
- Thumbnail images could be larger.
- Selected size and color indicators are not visually prominent enough.
- Sticky Add to Cart on mobile could have stronger visibility.
- Product highlights need better icon emphasis.
- Customer review cards feel slightly compact.
- Related product cards need stronger hover interactions.
- Product image zoom interaction can be smoother.

---
