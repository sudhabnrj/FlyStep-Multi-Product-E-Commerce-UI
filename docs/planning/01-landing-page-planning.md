# Planning Loop

---

# Planning Iteration 01

## Goal

Create a premium homepage focused on performance footwear.

### Observation

The homepage feels similar to many ecommerce templates.

### Critique

The hero lacks emotional impact.

Trust signals appear too low.

Category browsing is not emphasized.

### Refinement

Increase hero size.

Move trust bar directly below hero.

Add stronger headline.

Introduce featured categories earlier.

---

# Planning Iteration 02

## Goal

Improve conversion hierarchy.

### Observation

Too many competing sections.

Users may not know where to click.

### Critique

Primary CTA loses attention.

Product discovery happens too late.

### Refinement

Increase CTA prominence.

Reduce secondary actions.

Move Best Sellers above brand story.

Simplify copy.

---

# Planning Iteration 03

## Goal

Finalize homepage specification.

### Observation

Navigation, hero, trust, categories, products, testimonials and CTA now create a logical purchase journey.

### Critique

Newsletter should not interrupt product discovery.

Closing CTA should reinforce shopping intent.

### Final Refinements

Move Newsletter above Footer.

Strengthen final CTA.

Reduce unnecessary decorative elements.

Increase whitespace throughout the page.
