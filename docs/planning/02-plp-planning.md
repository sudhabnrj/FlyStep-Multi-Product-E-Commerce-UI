# Objective

Design a premium, responsive Product Listing Page (PLP) that allows users to quickly browse, filter, compare, and discover products with minimal effort.

The PLP should reduce decision fatigue while encouraging users to visit individual Product Detail Pages (PDP).

The page should feel similar to world-class ecommerce experiences while maintaining the FlyStep brand identity.

# Planning Loop

---

# Planning Iteration 01

## Goal

Design a clean, premium product browsing experience.

### Observation

The initial layout feels crowded due to excessive product information.

### Critique

Too many details compete for attention.

Filters appear overwhelming.

Product cards lack breathing room.

### Refinement

Increase card spacing.

Prioritize product image.

Reduce visible metadata.

Simplify filter layout.

---

# Planning Iteration 02

## Goal

Improve product discoverability.

### Observation

Users may struggle to compare products quickly.

### Critique

Price hierarchy is weak.

Ratings lack prominence.

Quick View is difficult to notice.

### Refinement

Increase price visibility.

Highlight ratings.

Improve Quick View button.

Enhance hover interactions.

---

# Planning Iteration 03

## Goal

Finalize the Product Listing Page specification.

### Observation

Navigation, filters, sorting, product cards and promotional content now create a smooth shopping experience.

### Critique

Recently Viewed should appear after the product grid.

Newsletter should remain near the footer.

Filter drawer requires optimization for mobile.

### Final Refinements

Improve mobile filter experience.

Increase white space.

Strengthen product card hierarchy.

Simplify promotional banner.
